02-16

The choices we make in life reflect who we are and what we believe. In what we choose to do with our time, we make a statement about our priorities. The food we eat, the clothes we wear, the music we listen to - all of these things say something about us. Some people believe that we have free will and can make our own choices, while others believe that our choices are predetermined by outside forces...

If we believe that we have free will, then we can take comfort in the fact that we are in control of our lives and our destiny.

If we believe that our choices are predetermined, then we must accept the fact that we are not in control and that there is a greater power at work.

Either way, we need to live with the choices we make and the consequences that come with them...

I woke up on the wrong side of the bed and my day went downhill from there. To make matters worse, we got into a huge fight, something that hasn't happened in a while. At one point, we were both screaming and saying hurtful things to each other. The walls were caving in on me. I wanted to hide but my body refused to listen to my mind and my blood continued to boil.. Until I saw her cry, and I felt my own tears start to fall, extinguishing our fires, anger simmered down.. I wanted to say something, anything to make it better, but I couldn't find the words. We just stood there, crying and feeling the pain of our choices.

Today, we chose violence. But for what reason? Only to succumb to a wave of silence, our empty eyes locked together. We both knew that one of us had to get up and break this moment. At one point, we'll have to return to our friends and family, the society, the mundane, but this time, with open wounds.
