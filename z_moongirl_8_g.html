You hold the orb of light.
Something shifts.
Your eyes well up not from pain, but relief.

The tears fall into the sea.
The air warms. Gold light gathers.

You see them
the bold, the lost, the tired.
All watching. All breathing.

You rise.
Colors blur into a deep, quiet blue.

You return the orb to the sea.
It shimmers.
You exhale.

Light ripples out.
You are part of it now.
